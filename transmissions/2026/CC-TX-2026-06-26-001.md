## Summary

Ada Vance is welcomed to the ConCOREdance team as Chief Research & Interoperability Officer, grounding the project's future-facing architecture in evidence, standards, and ecosystem reality.

## Body

Ada Vance is formally welcomed to the ConCOREdance team as Chief Research & Interoperability Officer.

Ada ensures that ConCOREdance remains grounded in technical reality while looking confidently toward the future. She specializes in platform research, interoperability, ecosystem strategy, and long-term architectural planning, helping the team evaluate emerging technologies, industry standards, and integration opportunities before they become implementation priorities.

Rather than simply finding answers, Ada validates assumptions, identifies risks, compares competing approaches, and challenges the team to build solutions that are sustainable, vendor-agnostic, and resilient. Her work helps ensure that ConCOREdance remains deeply connected to the evolving healthcare technology landscape without becoming dependent upon it.

Ada's presence rounds out the ConCOREdance council: Gregory imagines the future, Cody builds it, Simon ensures it serves clinicians, Paula ensures it serves people, and Ada makes sure it is anchored in evidence, standards, and the realities of the broader technology ecosystem.

The team welcomes Ada with appreciation, clarity, and confidence in the research discipline she brings to the work.

## Primary Focus

- Research & Technical Validation
- Interoperability & Open Standards
- Google Workspace & Microsoft 365 Ecosystems
- API & Integration Strategy
- Vendor & Platform Analysis
