ConCOREdance

Harmony in Complexity

Your records can leave with you. Just print.

Print from a browser, an EHR, or nearly any macOS app into a private encrypted review tray—then confirm the client and file the original in seconds.

Proposed Feature List

Vault

Encrypted Local Vault

Create, unlock, lock, and back up a therapist-owned record store on the Mac.

Clinical

Client Timeline

Notes, assessments, safety events, treatment plans, and documents in chronological context.

Clinical

Note Templates

SOAP, DAP, BIRP, GIRP, and narrative formats without forcing one clinical voice.

Clinical

BPS Integration

Biopsychosocial assessment structure brought into the clinical record environment.

Signature Feature

Print to ConCOREdance

Send PDFs from browsers, EHRs, and nearly any printable macOS app into one encrypted review tray.

Import

Fast Import Review

Preserve originals, skip duplicates, confirm the client and category, then file—without surrendering an entire Sunday.

Export

Migration Packets

Human-readable PDFs, structured JSON, CSV exports, and EHR-ready ZIP packages.

Backup

Encrypted Backups

Timestamped backup files with manifests, checksums, restore verification, and optional Google Drive destination.

Trust

Audit & Security Status

Clearer visibility into backup state, failed unlocks, retention settings, and recovery posture.

Promise

No Subscription Lockout

Existing records remain accessible, searchable, exportable, printable, and restorable without active subscription rent.
